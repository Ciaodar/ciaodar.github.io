Welcome to the underground! This mod introduces an immersive, illegal prisoner trade system. You can visit the backstreets of any town to find the "Captive Trading" area or interact with Lords directly on the map to buy their prisoners.

- **Native Trade UI**: Seamlessly buy prisoners using the native party screen.
- **Buy Captive Heroes**: Buy captive Lords from across the realm. They will be physically delivered to your party based on distance!
- **Map Encounters**: Talk to any Lord on the map to negotiate for their prisoners.
- **Moral Consequences**: Dealing in slaves grants Roguery XP, but your Honor will suffer and your Criminal Rating will soar.
