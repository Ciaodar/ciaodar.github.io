**Just Give Up Man! (JGUM)** makes surrender logic in Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord dynamic and realistic. No more grinding down every last starving defender; settlements now recognize hopeless situations and offer to surrender. Includes Field-lord and Patrol surrender logic with trait-based consequences.

- **Dynamic Settlement Surrender**: Context-aware surrender offers during sieges and starvation.
- **Siege Negotiation**: Proactive parley path with persuasion rounds and strategic consequences.
- **Lord & Patrol Surrender**: Outmatched enemies evaluate surrender based on strength and personality traits.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Full compatibility with Steam and Xbox Store folder structures.
