A Point-and-Click Adventure Through the Age of Innovation! Step into the shoes of a revolutionist inventor in an era of transformation. Your mission: bring forth groundbreaking inventions like electricity, light, and the automobile. Navigate the delicate web of political factions, gather support, and manage resources wisely.

- **Invent Revolutionary Technology:** Electricity, light bulbs, and cars await your genius.
- **Balance Faction Relations:** Keep different political groups satisfied to succeed.
- **Strategic Decision Making:** Every choice impacts your path to innovation.
- **Steampunk Aesthetic:** Immerse yourself in a beautifully crafted historical setting.
